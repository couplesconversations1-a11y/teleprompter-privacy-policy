Privacy Policy

Sytner Teleprompter — effective date: May 28, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Sytner Teleprompter app for macOS, developed by Ari Sytner.

Information We Collect

Sytner Teleprompter does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data — including session statistics, reading preferences, and script content — is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent to any server.

Microphone Access

The app requests access to your microphone solely to power the voice-tracking feature, which advances the teleprompter script as you speak. Microphone audio is processed locally on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Usage Data

The app maintains local statistics (session count, reading time, word frequency) for display in the in-app dashboard. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Sytner Teleprompter does not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs. There are no third parties with access to any information about your use of this app.

Data Retention

All data is stored in your macOS app sandbox. You can delete all app data at any time by removing the app from your Mac.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
